Lelam Privacy Policy

Transparency for our card game players.

Last Updated: January 2026

Lelam respects your privacy. We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from our users. The game operates locally or via minimal server interactions for multiplayer matchmaking without storing personal profiles.

Data Collection

We may collect anonymous usage data (e.g., game duration, crash reports) to improve app stability and performance. This data is not linked to your identity.

Multiplayer Interactions

During multiplayer matches, temporary connection data is used to synchronize game state between players. This data is ephemeral and is not retained after the match concludes.
