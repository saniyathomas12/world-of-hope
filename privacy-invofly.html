InvoFly Privacy Policy

Secure financial management for your peace of mind.

Last Updated: January 2026

InvoFly is designed to keep your business data local to your device whenever possible. We understand the sensitivity of financial data.

Data Storage

All invoice, catalog, and client data is stored locally on your device's internal storage or secure local database. We do not automatically upload your financial records to any cloud server.

Backups

If you choose to use cloud backup features (e.g., Google Drive, iCloud), data is encrypted before transmission and stored in your personal cloud account. We do not have access to these backups.
