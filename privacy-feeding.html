Feeding Counter Privacy Policy

Protecting your family's health data.

Last Updated: January 2026

Feeding Counter handles sensitive health data regarding your infant. We treat this data with the highest level of care.

Health Data Privacy

All tracking logs (feeding times, amounts, types) are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, and we do not sell or share your personal health information with third parties or advertisers.

Analytics

We do not use third-party analytics trackers that capture personal health information. Any analytical data is strictly aggregate and anonymous system performance data.
