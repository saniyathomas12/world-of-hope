Privacy Policy

Transparency and trust are at the core of our digital experiences.

01. Lelam

Last Updated: January 2026

Lelam respects your privacy. We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from our users. The game operates locally or via minimal server interactions for multiplayer matchmaking without storing personal profiles.

Data Collection

We may collect anonymous usage data (e.g., game duration, crash reports) to improve app stability and performance. This data is not linked to your identity.

02. InvoFly

Last Updated: January 2026

InvoFly is designed to keep your business data local to your device whenever possible. We understand the sensitivity of financial data.

Data Storage

All invoice and catalog data is stored locally on your device. If you use cloud backup features, data is encrypted before transmission.

03. GIF League

Last Updated: January 2026

GIF League allows users to share creativity. Privacy in a social environment is our priority.

User Content

GIFs created or shared during gameplay are temporary and typically processed for the duration of the game session. We do not permanently store user-generated content unless explicitly saved by the user.

04. Feeding Counter

Last Updated: January 2026

Feeding Counter handles sensitive health data regarding your infant. We treat this data with the highest level of care.

Health Data

All tracking logs (feeding times, amounts) are stored locally. We do not sell or share your personal health data with third parties.
