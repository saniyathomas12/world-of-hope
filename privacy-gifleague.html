GIF League Privacy Policy

Creativity and community, protected.

Last Updated: January 2026

GIF League allows users to share creativity. Privacy in a social environment is our priority.

User Content

GIFs created or shared during gameplay are temporary and typically processed for the duration of the game session. We do not permanently store user-generated content on our servers unless explicitly saved by the user to their gallery.

Community Guidelines

While we facilitate the sharing of content, we are not responsible for the nature of GIFs shared by other players in public lobbies. We encourage a respectful gaming environment.
